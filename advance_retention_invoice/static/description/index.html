Advance & Retention Invoice for Contracting

Manage Advance Payments and Retention Amounts on Invoices

Designed for Saudi Arabia & GCC Contracting Companies

Key Features

  • ✅ Add Advance Payment Amount field directly on customer & vendor invoices
  • ✅ Add Retention Amount field on invoices
  • ✅ Auto-creates balancing journal lines for advance and retention
  • ✅ Recalculates VAT 15% based on net amount after advance deduction
  • ✅ Full breakdown: Net Before VAT — VAT — Net Including VAT — Retention — Due Balance
  • ✅ Integrated with invoice report template
  • ✅ Supports Saudi Arabia VAT (ZATCA) requirements
  • ✅ Works on both Customer Invoices and Vendor Bills

Configuration Steps

  1. Install the module
  2. Go to each Customer / Vendor → Accounting tab
  3. Set the Advanced Account (e.g. 2000022 Advanced Account)
  4. Set the Retention Account (e.g. 111111101 Retention Account)
  5. On any invoice, enter the Advance Amount and/or Retention Amount
  6. Journal lines and totals are updated automatically

Partner Configuration

Set the Advance Account and Retention Account on each partner from the Accounting tab.

Invoice Form — Advance & Retention Fields

Advance Amount and Retention Amount fields appear directly on the invoice form.

Invoice Form — Retention Amount

Retention amount is deducted automatically and shown in the totals summary.

Journal Items — Advance Payment

A dedicated journal line is created automatically for the advance payment.

Journal Items — Retention Amount

A dedicated journal line is created automatically for the retention amount.

Printed Invoice Report

The printed tax invoice shows full breakdown including advance, retention, VAT, and due balance.

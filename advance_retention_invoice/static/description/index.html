Advance & Retention Invoice

Manage Advance Payments and Retention Amounts on Invoices

Features

  • Add Advance Payment Amount field on customer invoices
  • Add Retention Amount field on customer invoices
  • Auto-creates balancing journal lines for advance and retention
  • Recalculates VAT (15%) based on net amount after advance deduction
  • Shows full breakdown: Net Before VAT, VAT, Net Including VAT, Due Balance
  • Integrated with GCC invoice report template
  • Supports Saudi Arabia VAT (ZATCA) requirements

Configuration

After installation, go to each Customer/Vendor and set:

  • Advance Account — account used for advance payment journal lines
  • Retention Account — account used for retention journal lines
Then on any invoice, fill in the Advance Amount and/or Retention Amount fields found in the Other Info tab.

Dependencies

  • account
  • l10n_gcc_invoice
